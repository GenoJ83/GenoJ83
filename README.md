### [Venture](https://venture-steel.vercel.app/)
![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3776AB?style=for-the-badge&logo=python&logoColor=white)
![XGBoost](https://img.shields.io/badge/XGBoost-FF6F00?style=for-the-badge&logo=xgboost&logoColor=white)

![Firebase](https://img.shields.io/badge/Firebase-FFCA28?style=for-the-badge&logo=firebase&logoColor=black)

AI-powered investment advisory system for Kampala entrepreneurs. Predicts optimal business locations by analyzing foot traffic, nearby anchors (schools, markets, taxi stages), and competition within 400m. Uses XGBoost to forecast daily revenue and 12-month survival probability. Covers 5,000 ground-truth cells across 24 business types.
